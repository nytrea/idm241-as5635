IDM 241 - Microinteractions

Nike Shopping Preview Hover State

Triggers

  • Hovering over the area with the image and its text

Feedback

  • The cursor turns into a pointer
  • The information underneath the picture changes

Rules - While Hovering

  • Hover over the area with the image and text
  • The cursor turns into a pointer
  • The information underneath the picture changes from the name of the model to a image panel of its variations
  • New changes: A heart appears on the top right corner

Loops & Modes

  • When hovered on, the text underneath the image is replaced by a panel of style variations
  • A heart to be interacted with appears when the image is hovered on
