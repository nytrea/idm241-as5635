IDM 241 - Microinteractions

Nike Hover State Alpha Build

Triggers

  • Hovering over the area with the image
  • Hovering off of the area with the image

Rules - While Hovering

  • Hover over the area with the image and text
  • The cursor turns into a pointer
  • A heart eases in on the top right corner when on hover
  • The information panel under the image changes to a panel of style variations when on hover

Feedback

  • Hover over the area with the image and text
  • The cursor turns into a pointer
  • A heart eases in on the top right corner when on hover
  • The information panel under the image changes to a panel of style variations when on hover

Loops & Modes

  • When the image is hovered on, a heart to be interacted with appears
  • When the image is hovered on, another mode appears as the information of the shoe is replaced by a set of images of style variations
