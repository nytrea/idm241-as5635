Triggers

  • Hovering over and off of the entire area with the image and its text
  • Hovering over and off of the image
  • Hovering over and off of the 'Level up your game' link at the bottom
  • Clicking on the 'Level up your game' link at the bottom
  • Clicking on the image

Rules - While Hovering

    On Hover:

  • Over the image or link: the cursor turns into a pointer
  • Over the image or link: the gray drop shadow turns to a bright blue and enlarges
  • On the image: the image enlarges slightly while the cursor is on it
  • On the image: Right under the image, a bright blue light appears and is animated to slide left to right
  • Over the entire area: the drop shadow turns blue and enlarges
  • The link turns to a darker blue
  • An underline slides in and eases out when hovering on and off the link

    • On Click

  • When clicked on, the link becomes a lighter blue
  • The entire area's drop shadow becomes a dark gray
  • When clicked on, the image pulses once and expands; this expansion lowers in opacity. Clicking the image would take the user to a new webpage
