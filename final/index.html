Unity Category Button - Unity For All Industries

Triggers

  • Hovering over and off of the entire area with the image and its text
  • Hovering over and off of the link at the bottom
  • Clicking on the link at the bottom

Rules - While Hovering

  • Hover over the area with the image and text
  • The cursor turns into a pointer (over the image or the link)
  • On Hover: The image enlarges slightly through zoom-in
  • On Hover: The drop shadow turns blue and enlarges
  • When hovered on, the link turns to a darker blue
  • When clicked on, the link becomes a lighter blue and an underline slides in and then slides out
  • The link will direct users to a destination page after it is clicked
